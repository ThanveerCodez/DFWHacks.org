About DFWhacks

DFW Hacks is more than a hackathonâ€”it's a celebration of creativity, collaboration, and the future of tech. Join us on September 27th, 2025 at the Collin College Frisco for an action-packed day where high school students of all experience levels will form teams, build software and hardware projects, attend engaging workshops, and get guidance from industry mentors. Everyone is welcome! Come with an idea or simply your curiosity; leave with new skills, new friends, and something you're proud to demo.

Build

Build cutting-edge AI applications that solve meaningful problems and make a real impact in the world.

Connect

Work with students and mentors from across the Dallas-Fort Worth area!

Competition

Learn

Attend workshops, learn from experts, and master new technologies!
