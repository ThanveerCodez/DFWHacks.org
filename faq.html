Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about DFW Hacks

Who can participate?

Any high school student! Coding experience is required to ensure

When is it?

September 27, 2025 (updated from August 2). Watch the site and your email for the final schedule.

Where is it?

In North Dallas, TX. The exact venue will be shared with registered participants.

Is it free?

Yes! Thanks to our sponsors, DFW Hacks is free for all participants. Your ticket includes meals, swag, workshops, and mentor support.

What should I bring?

A laptop, charger, student ID, and anything you like to code or tinker with. We'll provide Wi-Fi, power, meals, and a beginner-friendly setup.

Do I need a team?

Nope! You can work alone, find a team or bring a team of up to 4. We'll help everyone find a group.

What can I build?

Anything that solves a real problem or showcases creativity—web or mobile apps, games, data/AI projects, or hardware hacks. You'll have access to mentors, workshops, and example starter kits to get going.

Are beginners supported?

Absolutely. We'll run hands-on workshops, office hours, and guided starter tracks so first-timers can ship a project by demo time.

Will there be food?

Yes—meals, snacks, and drinks throughout the event. We accommodate dietary restrictions; just tell us during registration.

Can I use AI tools?

Yes, as long as you follow our rules and disclose any AI assistance in your README . (See our Code of Conduct for details.)

How are projects judged?

By a panel of mentors and industry guests using criteria like impact, creativity, technical implementation, and presentation. Judges' decisions are final.

Do we keep our IP?

Yes—you own what you build. By demoing, you grant us permission to showcase your project for judging and event highlights.

What's the Code of Conduct?

We're committed to a safe, inclusive environment. Read our full Code of Conduct on the site. Violations may result in removal or disqualification.

How do I contact the team?

Email dfw.hackz@gmail.com or text/call +1 (972) 249-7263. We're happy to help!
